Our Focus

Why Dental Practices Specifically?

Dental is a high-consideration category. People don't book a root canal the way they order takeaway. They research, compare, ask questions, and then wait — sometimes for weeks — before committing. That gap between interest and booking is exactly where good AI makes the biggest difference. A patient who gets a thoughtful, instant response to their 10pm enquiry is far more likely to book than one who receives a reply three days later.

We focus exclusively on service-based businesses with high-value appointments — and dental practices are where we've seen the most consistent, measurable results.
